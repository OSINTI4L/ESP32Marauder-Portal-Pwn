Are you tired of local skids terrorizing your favorite starbucks with Flipper Zero evil portals, stopping you from enjoying your mocha latte and tiktok doom scrolling? Well why don't you pwn them?

For the lolz of course.

Here you will find walk-throughs for how to crash, deauth, and spam Flipper Zero evil portals, allowing you to finally take back your starbucks.

These attacks work on multiple ESP32 devices running Marauder, not just the Flipper Zero variant. When attacking those devices with the Hydra crash method you will need to change the IP address you are attacking as other devices do not consistantly spinup webservers on 172.0.0.1 like Flipper Zeros do.
